FAQ

1. What kind of games will Steazzy get for me?

Steazzy will search and purchase great games that you do not currently have in your library. This may be a game you’ve heard of or it may be a undiscovered gem. It’s an adventure.

2. Why can’t I select the catagorey of games I’m interested in?

Steazzy is still in it’s early stages, and has such is running very lean. We plan to expand the functionality as it grows based on the feedback of community members like you.

3. If I don’t like a game can I get a refund?

Unfortunately no. We comply with Steam’s official policy which does not allow for refunds. It is however, very easy to cancel your service with Steazzy if you are disatisfied.
